# Knights of Pen and Paper 2

Knights of Pen and Paper 2 is a turn-based, retro pixel-art adventure about chivalry, class warfare, off-beat pop references, and saving throws. It keeps the tabletop RPG framing while adding more danger, intrigue, and chaos to the party.
