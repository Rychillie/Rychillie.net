# The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Breath of the Wild turns Hyrule into a vast world built around discovery, exploration, and survival. Link climbs towers and mountains, crosses rivers, glides between peaks, battles enemies, hunts, scavenges equipment and resources, and cooks meals or elixirs to endure weather and recover from fights.
