# Bloodborne

Bloodborne sends you into the ancient city of Yharnam, a gothic place consumed by a strange illness and stalked by deranged crowds and nightmare creatures. Survival depends on aggressive, strategic combat, uncovering the city's darkest secrets, and facing chalice dungeons filled with traps, beasts, and rewards.
